# Output Contract

Use this contract whenever generating the final disclosure.

## Output order

Always present the final result in this section order:

1. 初拟的发明（或实用新型）名称
2. 名词解释
3. 所属技术领域
4. 背景技术
5. 发明创造的目的
6. 发明创造的技术方案以及具体实施例
7. 发明人认为要保护的发明内容的技术要点以及相应的有益效果
8. 附图
9. 其他

## Section requirements

### 1. 初拟的发明（或实用新型）名称
- Use a precise technical title.
- Prefer a concise, concrete title over a generic “一种智能方法”.

### 2. 名词解释
- Define important technical terms and abbreviations.
- Normalize synonyms into one canonical term.

### 3. 所属技术领域
- State the direct technical field or application field.
- Keep it concise.

### 4. 背景技术
Include:
- the closest plausible prior art,
- the main technical problem,
- why the problem arises.

Do not use business goals as the main problem.

### 5. 发明创造的目的
Summarize:
- the technical problem solved,
- the core technical means,
- the technical effect achieved.

### 6. 发明创造的技术方案以及具体实施例
Include:
- use scenario,
- core technical solution,
- at least two embodiments or variants by default,
- alternatives or substitutions,
- beneficial effects.

Explain mechanism, structure, relationships, or workflow rather than only desired outcomes.

### 7. 发明人认为要保护的发明内容的技术要点以及相应的有益效果
- Rank technical points from most important to least important.
- For each point, explain the advantage relative to prior art.
- Keep points concrete enough to support future claims.

### 8. 附图
Provide useful figure suggestions such as:
- system architecture diagram,
- module relationship diagram,
- process flow diagram,
- sequence diagram,
- structural diagram,
- deployment diagram.

### 9. 其他
Provide optional references, standards, keywords, or retrieval hints that may help later drafting or review.

## Completion rules

The result is incomplete if any of the following is missing:
- background technology,
- technical problem,
- invention purpose,
- concrete technical solution,
- at least two embodiments or variants,
- protectable technical points,
- drawing suggestions,
- the 其他 section.
